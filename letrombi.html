Fanny: She lived in the favelas of Rio de Janeiro to stay hidden from the authorities. As a child she already broke the security systems of many governments to acces their secret databases. Morgan: aka Margo. Don't be fooled by this always smiling face. He is the son of the greatest godfather in Sicilia. He has always been safe thanks to his father and the worst crimes he was responsible for where nothing to him. After some time he began to feel a lack of adrenaline and decided to go the outer world and survive on his own. His then heard of a filthy place where he would be able to test his willpower. Does he have what it takes ? Maybe, maybe not.
